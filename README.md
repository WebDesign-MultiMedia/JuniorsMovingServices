# Junior's Moving Services

A single-page marketing website for **Junior's Moving Services**, a moving and relocation
company offering residential, commercial, local, and long-distance moving — plus add-on
services like furniture assembly, TV mounting, packing, labor, and dump runs.

The site introduces the business, lists services, and gives visitors two ways to reach out:
a contact section (email/phone) and a quote request form.

## Pages / Sections

- **Home** — hero intro with calls to action to request a quote or call directly.
- **Services** — grouped list of moving services and additional services.
- **Contact** — email and phone contact cards.
- **Quote** — a form for visitors to request a free moving quote (submits via HeroTofu).

## Tech

Plain HTML/CSS with [Bootstrap 5](https://getbootstrap.com/) (via CDN) for the nav
component. No build step — just open `index.html` or serve the folder statically.
